About Us

Royale Cold Storage operates two refrigerated warehouses, Royale Cold Storage (Taytay, Rizal) and Royale Cold Storage North (Marilao, Bulacan). We serve customers who require chiller rooms, freezer rooms, and blast freezing services. We are a full-service inventory management company that offers systematic picking and packing as well as load planning.

Our Services

No matter what you might need in cold storage logistics services, Vertical Cold Storage has a solution for you. Our veteran team members are industry experts that can find a custom solution for your storage and logistics operations, ensuring that your needs are met to your precise specifications.

Blast Freezing

To preserve your product’s integrity, quality, and food safety based on the strict USDA and FDA guidelines.

Crossdocking

To eliminate the expensive hassle of storing items in different warehouses during transportation between locations. Crossdocking increases the speed at which goods are loaded, scanned, and unloaded for their next route.

Layer and Case Picking

To meet the dynamic needs of your customers in the quantities they require.

Import and Export Services

To provide document processing and regulatory inspection services for your global business.

Temperature Integrity

Our refrigerated warehouses maintain the proper temperatures to keep products fresh, but we go the extra mile: every corner of each facility has the same optimal conditions. From the moment a pallet enters our warehouse to the time it is loaded onto a truck, intermodal container, or railcar, it is all in a temperature-controlled space operated by expert handlers. Rest assured that there are absolutely no cold chain breaches at any point with Vertical Cold Storage solutions.

Food Safety

We use every means possible to minimize risks in food storage. Maintaining the safety of the food products is our top priority. Our skilled staff takes great care to create a safe environment and have advanced recall processes in place.
